## Our Commitment

Every comparison on this site follows three rules:

1. **No sponsored scores.** We do not accept payment for scores, verdicts, or placement. If a tool pays us, it gets a disclosure — not a better score.

2. **All data is traceable.** Every score is backed by a cited source: public benchmarks, community consensus, official documentation, or disclosed hands-on testing.

3. **Methodology is public.** Our scoring framework, weight adjustments, and test methods are documented in every article and on our [About](/about/) page. If you disagree with a weight, you can see exactly how it affected the total score.

## How Articles Are Created

### Selection
We prioritize tool pairs based on:
- Search demand (are people actively comparing these two tools?)
- Decision difficulty (is the choice non-obvious?)
- Data availability (can we find enough reliable information to make a meaningful comparison?)

### Research
Each article draws from:
- **Public benchmarks:** LMSYS Chatbot Arena, HumanEval, SWE-bench, Video Arena, and industry evaluations
- **Community consensus:** Reddit, Hacker News, official forums, professional communities
- **Official documentation:** Pricing pages, technical docs, release notes
- **Hands-on testing:** Identical prompts run across tools and compared side-by-side

When we use community consensus or benchmarks instead of hands-on testing, we disclose it in the article's "Data Sources" section.

### Review
- Every article is checked for factual accuracy before publishing
- Pricing tables are verified against official sources
- Scores are cross-referenced with available benchmark data

## Updates

| Update Type | Frequency |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Pricing** | Quarterly, or when announced |
| **Scores** | When major model updates are released |
| **New data** | As reliable benchmarks and community data become available |
| **Corrections** | Within 48 hours of receiving a verified correction |

The "Last updated" date at the bottom of each article reflects the most recent substantive update.

## Corrections

If you find an error:
- [Open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/Linanxi12/aitoolscompare/issues) with the article URL and the correction
- We verify and update within 48 hours
- Major corrections are noted at the bottom of the article

## Independence

- **Funding:** This site is independently operated. Revenue comes from affiliate commissions and (future) display advertising.
- **No pay-to-play:** Scores cannot be purchased. Verdicts cannot be influenced by payment.
- **Transparent methodology:** If we change how we score, we update the public framework and note the change.
