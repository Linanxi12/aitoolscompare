## The Shared DNA

Stable Diffusion 3 and Flux share a history. The core research team behind SD (Robin Rombach, Andreas Blattmann, and colleagues) created Stable Diffusion at Stability AI — the model that launched the open-source AI image revolution. In 2023, they left Stability AI to found Black Forest Labs and build Flux — their second attempt, informed by everything they learned from SD's successes and limitations.

SD3 represents Stability AI's continued development of the original line. Flux represents what the original creators built when they could start fresh. Comparing them is comparing the original vision evolved (SD3) against the original vision reimagined (Flux).

## 3 Key Tests 🔬

### Test 1: Image Quality

**Prompt:** Photorealistic coffee shop interior. **Flux:** Better lighting, more realistic textures, cleaner details. **SD3:** Competent but slightly flatter lighting, more "AI artifacts" on complex textures. Flux's hybrid diffusion-transformer architecture is genuinely better than SD3's architecture for photorealism.

<div class="verdict-box"><div class="verdict-label">📝 Verdict</div><p class="verdict-text"><strong>Flux — better architecture produces better images.</strong></p></div>

### Test 2: Text Rendering

**Prompt:** Storefront with "FRESH BAKERY — EST. 2026" sign. **Flux:** Text perfectly rendered, correct spelling, proper perspective. This is the hardest task in AI image gen — Flux handles it better than any model (including Midjourney and DALL-E). **SD3:** Garbled text — "FRESH BAKRY" with inconsistent letter sizing. SD3's text rendering is the old generation's standard; Flux's is the new benchmark.

<div class="verdict-box"><div class="verdict-label">📝 Verdict</div><p class="verdict-text"><strong>Flux wins decisively — best text rendering in the category.</strong></p></div>

### Test 3: Ecosystem

**SD3:** Thousands of community models on Civitai, Hugging Face. ControlNet, LoRAs, IP-Adapter — rich extension ecosystem. Vast tutorial library, established tooling (Automatic1111, ComfyUI). **Flux:** Newer, smaller ecosystem. Growing fast but still 2-3 years behind SD's community depth. For technical workflows relying on specific extensions: SD3 still wins.

<div class="verdict-box"><div class="verdict-label">📝 Verdict</div><p class="verdict-text"><strong>SD3 on ecosystem; Flux catching up fast.</strong></p></div>
