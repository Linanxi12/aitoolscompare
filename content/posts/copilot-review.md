## Three Scenario Tests 🔬

### Scenario 1: Code Completion & Quality

Reliable, polished completions. Single-line accuracy is excellent; multi-line is good but shorter than Cursor's (2-3 lines vs 5-10). For daily coding in VS Code or JetBrains: consistent and productive.

### Scenario 2: Ecosystem & IDE Support

Works everywhere — VS Code, JetBrains, Neovim, and GitHub.com. Native PR review with AI suggestions. Copilot Workspace for issue-to-PR workflows. No other AI code assistant has this breadth of platform integration.

### Scenario 3: Enterprise Readiness

SOC 2 compliance, IP indemnity, admin controls, and usage analytics. For regulated industries and large organizations: Copilot is the only option with enterprise-grade compliance at scale.
