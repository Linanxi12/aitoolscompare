- **Integrated** — already in ChatGPT, no separate subscription needed
- **API available** — build image gen into your apps

### Summary

- **Midjourney v7 is the best AI image generator for artistic quality.** If you care about how an image *feels*, Midjourney is unmatched.
- **DALL-E 3 is the best for practical, text-accurate images.** If you need a logo, poster, or diagram with readable text, DALL-E 3 delivers.
- **For most creators, the combination is ideal:** Midjourney for hero images and art, DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT for quick graphics and text-heavy images.
- **Budget tip:** At $10/mo Midjourney Basic vs $20/mo ChatGPT Plus (includes DALL-E 3), Midjourney is cheaper for pure image generation if you can stay within ~200 images/mo.
