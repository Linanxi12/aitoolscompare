### Test 2: Genre Range

10 genres tested. **Suno** handled all 10 competently — pop, hip-hop, country, metal all credible. **Udio** excelled at electronic, ambient, pop — weaker on country, metal, reggae. Suno's training data is broader; Udio's is deeper but narrower.
