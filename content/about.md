## Why AI Tools Compare?

Every week, dozens of new AI tools launch. Keeping up is exhausting. **AI Tools Compare** cuts through the noise with hands-on, side-by-side comparisons that answer one question: *Which tool should you use for your specific task?*

## How We Test

Every comparison on this site follows the same methodology:

1.  **Define the task** — coding, writing, image generation, data analysis, etc.
2.  **Run the same prompts/inputs** across all tools being compared.
3.  **Score across dimensions**: accuracy, speed, ease of use, price, and special features.
4.  **Declare a winner** for each use case — no "it depends" cop-outs (though we explain the trade-offs).
